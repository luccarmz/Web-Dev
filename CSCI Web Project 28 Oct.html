In this we page I will be sharing my knowledge about working out and what is the best and moost effective way to change our lifes for the best.

Lets begin by thinking what our goal might be and what kind of diet we would need for each one of our goals:

Gain Muscle: For this categorie our main goals will be to be on a Surplus metabolic rate and do Hypertrophy training.

Loose fat: For this categorie our main goal will be to be on a Deficit metabolic rate and although its not nessesary I really encourage people trying to loose weight to also do hypertrophy training so they dont loose as much muscle.

Now you might be asking what is a Surplus and a Deficit?

Surplus:On nutritional facts surplus means the person will have to eat more than what is required or necessary.

Deficit:On nutritional facts deficit means the person will have to eat less than what is required or necessary.

How to find our Metabolic Rate?

Your basal metabolism rate is produced through the following basal metablic rate formula: Men: BMR = 66 + (6.23 × weight in pounds) + (12.7 × height in inches) − (6.8 × age in years) Women: BMR = 655 + (4.3 x weight in pounds) + (4.7 x height in inches) – (4.7 x age in years), or you can use a metabolic rate calculator like this one : -Metabolic Rate Calculator-

After plugging our information in this formula and doing some simple math we finally get our BMR Now you might ask, What do I need my BMR for? and the answer is that with this we will now be able to get our Surplus and Deficit data required for us to achieve our goals.

Now what we are gonna do is grab our BMR data and multiply it by our activity factor which is:

-Activity Factor-

  • Sedentary = 1.1
  • Lightly Active = 1.3
  • Moderatly Active = 1.5
  • Very Active = 1.7

After multiplying our BMR with our Activity Factor we get our TEE (Total Energy Expenditure) and with this we have every information necessary.

Depending on what our goal is we will either add or subtract from 300 to 600 to our TEE number. If our goal is a Surplus we add otherwise if our goal is a Deficit we subtract.

WARNING!!! It is NOT recommendable at all to go below 600 if you do this can bring some health problems.
